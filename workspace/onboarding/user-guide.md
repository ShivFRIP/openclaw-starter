# Vishy User Guide — FRIP Team

Welcome to Vishy, FRIP's AI research assistant! This guide will help you get started.

## What is Vishy?

Vishy (short for Vishwakarma) is an AI assistant built for FRIP. It's available 24/7 on Telegram and can help with policy research, document drafting, data analysis, and more.

## Getting Started

1. Open Telegram and search for your bot
2. Send any message — Vishy will introduce itself and start helping
3. That's it! No passwords, no setup, no installation.

## What Vishy Can Do

**Research & Analysis**
- Search the web for the latest policy developments in India
- Analyze government notifications, court rulings, and regulatory changes
- Compare policies across states or countries
- Summarize long documents and research papers

**Document Drafting**
- Draft policy briefs, memos, and analysis reports
- Create executive summaries
- Help structure arguments and recommendations
- Proofread and improve existing drafts

**Knowledge Base**
- Look up files in your shared knowledge base on Google Drive
- Find documents across all research projects
- Upload new research and deliverables to the right folders

**General Help**
- Answer questions about your research areas
- Brainstorm ideas for policy positions
- Help with data interpretation

## Example Prompts

Here are some things you can ask:

- "What are the latest policy changes on [topic]?"
- "Summarize [document/article]"
- "Draft a policy brief on [topic] for [audience]"
- "Find recent news on [topic]"
- "What files do we have on [project]?"

## Tips for Better Results

1. **Be specific** — "India SWM rules 2026 changes" works better than "tell me about waste"
2. **Provide context** — "For our policy brief on import duties, summarize the key arguments for reducing tariffs on solar panels"
3. **Ask follow-ups** — The assistant remembers your conversation within a session
4. **Request formats** — "Give me bullet points" or "Write this as a formal memo"
5. **Say "help"** — Anytime you're unsure, just type "help" for guidance

## Privacy & Cost

- **Private conversations:** Your chat is separate from other team members. No one else sees your messages.
- **No cost to you:** The assistant uses free AI models for most tasks. Your admin manages the infrastructure.
- **Data stays within your systems:** Conversations and files remain within your organization's systems.

## Need Help?

- Type **"help"** in your chat for a quick capabilities reminder
- Contact your admin for technical issues or if the bot is not responding
